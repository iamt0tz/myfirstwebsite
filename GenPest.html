Problem with household pests? We can help.


We are here to protect you and your property from the ill effects of a pest infestation. We control the proliferation of ants, cockroaches, rodents, mosquitoes, flies and other common pests that constantly bother you.

Target Professional Pest Control are well trained in general pest control management and pest elimination. Using modern treatment tools and environment-friendly products, we will track down all household pests in and around your property and help you eliminate these problems.

We always start with a thorough inspection of your facility to look for evidences of pest infestation. Once we have identified the problem, we prescribe the appropriate treatment. Even after we have serviced your property, Target Professional Pest Control technicians are always on hand to provide tips on how to keep these pests away.
